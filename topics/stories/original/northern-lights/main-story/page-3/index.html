The next day

Aurora Borealis

Auri woke up with her arms and legs wrapped around her Kitsune Miku pillow. She was face to face with the vocaloid. I wonder what types of dreams I was having, she thinks, then laughs it off. She’s never had a sexual thought in her life. Why would she, when she never had genitals or hormones in her life? They supplemented her with gene changes instead of using hormones. Their reason was that hormones were far too bulky and outdated. She also thought they wouldn’t know what it would do to her if they did introduce a natural hormone into a completely artificial organism. Well, you didn’t miss what you never had, she mused. She never cared for those pleasures. The idea of them were quite alien to her. And she accepted that about herself. There was a lot she was confident in and didn’t really feel the need to worry about it. Sometimes that’s just how furries were born. Or in her case, made. Maybe sometimes she wished for someone to be close to. But meeting someone like Yuki made her feel like she found it. She had no need for it to be romantic. Just a relationship was something she desired. And that’s precisely why she got up and felt so excited for a new day at school. To see her new friend again!

She looked at herself in the full-body mirror again. Her unclothed body was truly a masterpiece. The scientists got everything right. She put her paws over the lack of breasts and sighed. Oh well. But scanning up and down the length of her body, she felt content as who she is. She slipped into her skirt and tossed the sailor top over her head. She was feeling pretty today, so she got the crop topped shirt. She went into her bathroom and saw an untouched makeup kit laying there. It was a creationday gift from her sponsor, from her most recent creationday celebration. She will call it birthday if anyone asks her about that date, but her and the scientists know that the date is simply the day they chose to start her heart for the first time. She was baby sized when they did so and she developed normally after that. But they failed many times before finally getting it right with 193. She opened the kit. A wonderfully luxurious “outer space” pallet. Girls would kill for this pallet. He must have got it as a play on her space-affiliated name. She loved her unique name. It fit her hair, which she is absolutely sure that the scientists did it on purpose. If the name came first or it was the hair first, she will never know. She dabbed the sparkly, stellar makeup and it made her cheeks glisten. Not too much, because she’s already seen too many anime shows that have girls who go crazy with makeup at school. She doesn’t wish to be a laughing stock, but she does want to blow furries away. What makes today special? She just felt good, inexplicably so. She carefully and gently applied the meteorite black eyeliner in the current trend of wings that extend out. This trend must have come and gone quite a few times. She swears Egyptian hieroglyphics have these wings on the ladies eyes. Or they are just impressionistic eyelashes. But it certainly looks good. She made them small, to fly under the radar. Not wanting to be bold. But maybe a little showing off wasn’t bad. Even the small amount of meteorite eyeliner she applied sparkles. The cosmic blush certainly had a shimmer to it as well. The pinkish tones look absolutely wonderful over the light blue fur on her cheeks. Yuki-Chan was going to love it.

Auri hurried and put her purse in her book bag. She slung it over her shoulder and clicked the button to wake the car up. She forgot she wanted to get to school early to find a solution for her math and science class. Or at least talk to her homeroom teacher about it before class started. She needed to make the early bus, or else she would be on time and have to wait her turn to talk to the teacher, which could take half the class time. The purr of the auto-driving car got louder, and she could tell it came out of parking and stopped at the front for her. She took a last look for things she needed and was glad she did when she saw art supplies laying out. They weren’t the portable ones she wanted, but the big bulky ones will have to do for now, just in case they weren’t able to solve her math and science problem just yet. Dumping these items in her available pocket of her book sling, she made it out the door and got in the passenger seat. It already knew to take her to the bus stop. She guessed she would have to program it to take her anywhere else. She planned to, the minute it was eligible for roads. The early bus was the friendliest she’s seen so far. With only 3 other furries, she could breathe easy. It was just something about crowds that really made her uneasy. This was much more manageable. Hopefully when she gets home, her deliveries will be waiting for her.

Her homeroom teacher looked at her. “You think the class is too easy for you?” Auri explained she was homeschooled and her math and science was much farther along than what was being taught. Her homeroom teacher suggested a placement test. Auri would find the most advanced placement test she could. An actual challenge. She forked through the placement test pile in the admin’s office. She passed up Geometry, Algebra, and then stopped on statistics. She didn’t know that, so she went back a math class. The second part of Algebra. That sounded challenging to her. It would let her skip… almost 5 years of schooling. That sounded like a good deal to her. Now for science. Earth science, Astronomy, all much too easy for her. She finally stopped at Chemistry and went back one. Biology. That could be a fun challenge for her. It would let her skip four years. She brought these two placement tests to the admin, and the well-dressed feline raised an eyebrow at her.

“Your homeschooling taught you all the way to these classes? I would like to speak with them on the phone before giving you these tests.”

Auri nodded and gave them her sponsor’s landline by memory. They typed the phone number into their smartphone and put a bluetooth speaker on their ear. Auri had the same type of feline ear, she knew how impossible phones could be without bluetooth. Phones were a peculiar object. Without the invention of bluetooth, they would have to be made insanely large and cumbersome to be effective. That or just have it on speaker all the time. But Auri’s hearing could pick up most noises if she focused. She decided to eavesdrop on the whole conversation. Both ends of the conversation. She could even hear the bluetooth speaker that was clipped onto the cat’s ear.

“Hello, you have reached Musake-Sama’s line. He is currently in an important call. Do you want me to leave a message for him?” The secretary answered. Hopefully Musake-Sama will be done with his call soon.

“I wish to speak with him directly. I can hold.” The cat tapped his claw on the desk and looked up at Auri. “Is Musake-Sama a relative of yours? His title sounds important.” Auri got quiet at a question like this. She didn’t really know how to answer that and wasn’t prepared. She cleared her throat.

“He homeschooled me and directed my education.” She didn’t want to go into any more. How would I even explain that he funded my existence? Luckily the line went active again and the cat returned to the call.

“Hello, this is Watashi School calling. We would like to verify that Auri has learned up to Biology and Algebra II before having her take the placement test. Did you homeschool her up to that point?” The cat was instantly more formal, he could tell he was dealing with someone important.

“Yes, that would be correct. She should know bits of Chemistry and Statistics as well and has mastered everything leading up to those classes. Feel free to test her on any subjects if you feel it necessary.” Auri could catch most everything said out of the small bluetooth clip on the cat’s ear. She was surprised at how well she could pinpoint her hearing. It’s possible that since she knew where the sound would come from, her ears could align themselves to catch the most amount of feedback from the small speaker. She hoped she didn’t look too peculiar with her ears perked up and aimed at the furry adult in front of her. But she knew that he wouldn’t suspect that she could eavesdrop. His ears were sensitive, so his Bluetooth volume was most likely at a very quiet amplitude. But he couldn’t imagine how perceptive her own ears were. Many times as accurate. And she always felt a bit self conscious about her strangely large cat ears. They looked like cat ears, but if you compared her to any other feline, hers were taller, almost closer to a doberman pinscher. She found herself tucking them away because it made sounds more bearable, but also because she didn’t need to look more unusual than she already did. But she was feeling pretty today, so nothing would stop her! Unless things got really loud, then she would don a cute beanie she brought along. The admin let her take the test and she brought them back faster than he would of thought she would take. She won’t even be late to homeroom, he noticed. He passed the tests through the scanner and it graded her test with more than passable marks. She certainly was able to move past those subjects.

“Alright, you have shown your understanding of these subjects. Assuming you know all the previous ones, would you like to be enrolled in a senior high school program?” Auri asked him if that would remove her from the school, and when he said only for the last 2 hours, she shook her head.

“Is there a way to keep the class I’m in, but take a harder course?” He scrunched his eyebrows at this question.

“You mean an online course? You may be taught online and I won’t keep you from staying in the class for those 2 hours. So you may do the online anywhere you like, if you have a laptop and an internet connection.” Auri perked up at this offer. Yes! I can stay next to Yuki!

“I would love that, thank you.” He said he would inform her teacher, Tsubaki-San, about it. He had her use his computer to enroll in the accelerated course and create an account.

“Now use that login info on any device to take the classes. You aren’t required to attend your last 2 hours now, but may stay if you like. Make sure those classes are halfway finished before the semester ends and that you finish the courses completely by the end of the year. This is self paced, so there is more responsibility on you to keep up with it. Oh and one more thing. You will take a physical test at the end of the year, sent from the Senior High School. This will only be for our statements, but you do need to pass it in order to get full credit for taking these courses.” And with all that, he turned the monitor back to face him and she excused herself after thanking him. It worked out perfectly, she celebrated with joy, now I need to ask for yet another package from Musake-Sama. But she felt he would be joyous to see that she is accelerating her learning and taking more responsibility. So he would be more than happy to send her a laptop.

Asuna, Yuki

Yuki walked into the classroom. She didn’t see Auri on the morning transport, but lo and behold, Auri was already in her seat. She must have caught the early bus. Yuki took a seat next to her. She greeted her friend cheerily and Auri matched her enthusiasm. Yuki’s jaw dropped. She froze and Auri noticed her look. Before she could ask what was going through Yuki’s mind, she blurted it out.

“You look absolutely gorgeous!” Yuki has never been able to say her mind like that. It just came out. She saw Auri’s face glow with happiness. Oh my gosh, that makes her even prettier! Yuki was astonished at her own boldness. Auri looked so happy when she heard the compliment, it made Yuki want to say more.

“I’m falling in love with your makeup,” Yuki never has worn makeup, but holy moly did that makeup work on Auri. Students around were agreeing with her and all the girls asked what pallet she used. She shyly told them that she used the “Outer Space” pallet from Cosmic Creations. Some girls who knew the brand swooned and ask her how she got her paws on stuff like that.

“I got it for my 12th cre- my 12th birthday.” The students, and Yuki, complimented her use of the pallet. Auri was so unused to the attention. Even some boys said they liked the glittery look it gave off. The attention was brought off her when the teacher started the class. Homeroom was a nice period to have. It was where you could get fursonal help from the teacher on any subject. Students typically used it to work on homework, or more accurately, slack off. Homeroom has some of its own requirements. But they were easy, like book reports or other fun activities and projects. And it still only being the second day, there wasn’t much to do.

Yuki was glad that she got Auri back to herself again. She could tell that Auri got nervous from all the attention.

“I get nervous when too many furries are paying attention to me as well,” she confided in Auri. Auri looked like she was getting over it though and smiled at her again. Yuki always wondered what went on in Auri’s mind. She seemed so shy with other furries, but was totally comfortable with Yuki. That made her happy. It let Yuki become comfortable, too.

“Yeah, and thank you for complimenting me, Yuki-Chan. It made my day!” Yuki noticed how Auri singled out Yuki’s compliment to the reason her day was made. Maybe trying to tell her that Yuki’s opinion was the one that mattered. Yuki’s heart swelled and she hugged her friend.

“I like you because you’re gorgeous on the inside, as well.” The words just came to Yuki’s lips. Uh oh, I hope I didn’t make her cry. She didn’t know if this would make her cry like yesterday’s events did. Maybe Auri was holding it in because she didn’t want to streak the makeup. She didn’t end up crying, but Auri sure hugged back. The tight hug was one only a close friend could give. The’ve become close friends faster than Yuki has ever made a friend. It just clicked like that.

Homeroom was by in a flash and the Japanese/English teacher walked in. Auri got serious with her notebook, no doubt trying to catch up. Yuki forgot to ask about the tutoring. When she asked Auri, she instantly lit up and excitedly told her she was allowed to go to Yuki’s house. Yuki was happy, but only really wanted to go to Auri’s house. She asked about that, but Auri shook her head sadly.

“I don’t think I can have furries over, unfortunately. But we can go anywhere we want after school, it doesn’t have to be your house if you don’t want,” Auri was perceptive. She must have seen the look that Yuki made when Auri said she could go to Yuki’s.

“We could go to the library… or the park,” Yuki starts to think of fun places to hang out. Auri tells her they sound wonderful. Yuki cheers up again. I’d have to tell my mom that I’ll be home later, but if she doesn’t need me to babysit my brothers that should be fine with her. She devises a plan to make this work as smoothly as possible, “We can hang out today, right?” Auri nods and still has that amazing smile on her face.

Yuki watches Auri-Chan as she looks back down at her paper, her flowing hair brushed to one side of her head to prevent it from covering her paper. It droops past the desk and sways to an invisible light breeze. She scribbles her neat Japanese letters, writing quickly to capture the content Murake-Sensei is droning on about. Yuki tries not to get caught staring at her friend, and looks back down at her paper. It is almost empty because this is just review so far. She doodles on her paper. Before she knew it, she had drawn Auri working at her desk. Yuki blushed deeply as Auri looked over, and she hoped that it didn’t come off as weird.

“Hey, that is awesome! I love drawing, too,” Auri breaks into a grin. Yuki’s heart flutters and the compliment strikes her speechless. She smiles back and imagines how fun it would be to draw with Auri.

“Maybe we could draw after school today. I brought my supplies,” Auri practically read her mind. Yuki nodded shyly, already getting excited.

The class passed fast for Yuki. Auri claims the time slows down the instant the teacher walks in, but Yuki didn’t have to pay attention and her mind was elsewhere. She was thinking about hanging out with Auri everyday after school. They could listen to music and draw together and get to know each other more. Yuki is surprised how comfortable Auri already is with her. For a furry that never made any friends, she sure seemed natural around Yuki.

The Science/Math teacher Tsubaki-San came in after a brief interlude where the students were able to eat and chat. Auri got up to talk to her before the class went in session. Yuki wasn’t able to hear what they were talking about, but wanted to ask Auri when she came back to her desk. Auri waltzed back to her desk. Yuki never noticed, but Auri had an air to her posture and stance, and it showed up how she walked. It was as if she grew up with very traditional parents. Yuki wondered how Auri’s parents were like. Auri had a very formal way of writing too, so she assumes that they must have been very strict.

“The material was too easy for me, so I’ll be taking online classes,” Auri sees Yuki’s look, “but don’t worry, I can take them in here.” Yuki’s emotions ran rampant as she realized that Auri purposely wanted to stay in the class for her. She could take online anywhere, but she wanted to be next to Yuki. Her heart pounded with a frightening new strength. What was this feeling? Her body was motionless, she couldn’t even smile like she wanted to. Auri must have noticed and she continued to tell her about it.

“I’m glad that I’m able to stay in the class to take it. I was frightened they would take me out of the class when I wanted more difficult material.” Auri said to a frozen Yuki. But to top it all off, the light blue furry added, “I didn’t want them to take me out because then I wouldn’t get to see you for as long.” And this finally broke Yuki out of it as she forced herself to utter her thanks and instantly her smile came back to life. She breathlessly stood up to hug Auri, but stumbled and Auri caught the falling furry in her arms. This brought them much closer than they usually were and Yuki’s face flushed. Auri smiled in amusement by her friend being so flustered. Her strong arms picked the frail girl and set her back on her hind paws.

“Careful now,” She said to Yuki, seemingly unaware of the class watching the two. Yuki was painfully conscious of the eyes on her, and she retreated into a shy ball, sitting down and curling up at her desk. Her mind was now thinking at lightning speed, compared to her freezing up. She now replayed what happened over and over. It was something directly out of anime. And Yuki was the star-struck girl in love with her classmate. Wait, did she just admit to that? Everything about it clicked. It was why she was so intrigued by the mysterious blue furry, why she loved that Auri would go out of her way to stay in the class with her, why she wanted to hug her all the time. No, her mind had to slow down. It was rampant with the Anime romance comedies. Those were influencing her to dream about it, making her connect dots that weren’t there. Yes, that is rational. She had always watched the Dandere characters and related to their shyness. She also craved to have someone that she would fall in love with and that they would notice her. This is just her wishing that life was like anime, that is all.

Yuki looks up and sees that class has moved on without her. Auri is drawing since she gets to take classes online now. Yuki gets the bravado to look over at her paper, to see what she is drawing. She is blown away by how amazing the drawing is, it is so close to anime, it could be professional. But that isn’t the most astonishing thing about it even. The character is Yuki! This furry will never stop surprising Yuki, again and again. She smiles to herself and tells Auri that she loves it. Auri looks up and smiles back, “She’s beautiful, isn’t she?” Yuki giggles at this roundabout compliment.

The rest of the class flies by, and Yuki is surrounded in a haze of happiness. She is so glad to have met Auri and wishes to know everything about her. She is also excited to draw with her after school. She is a magnificent artist and could teach Yuki a thing or two. The class bell finally rings and the students pack their bags. Yuki shows Auri where the library is, and to both their surprise, Yuki grabs Auri’s paw to walk paw-in-paw with her down the isles. Auri just goes with it. Yuki wonders, what is going on in her mind…

Aurora

Aurora feels a soft paw wrap around hers. She looks over at Yuki, who makes eye contact and then breaks it just as fast. What an interesting girl! She is very shy, a complete social mystery to Auri.
